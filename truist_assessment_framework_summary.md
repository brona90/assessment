# Truist Bank Technology Assessment Framework - Executive Summary

## Framework Overview

The Truist Bank Technology Assessment Framework provides a comprehensive evaluation methodology for assessing maturity across four critical technology domains: Site Reliability Engineering (SRE), Automation, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Audit capabilities. This framework is specifically designed for banking regulatory compliance while incorporating industry best practices and emerging technology standards.

## Assessment Architecture

### Four-Domain Structure

**1. Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) - 25% Weight**
- Reliability Engineering Practices
- Monitoring and Observability
- Incident Management
- Capacity Planning and Performance

**2. Automation Capabilities - 25% Weight**
- Infrastructure Automation
- Application Deployment
- Testing Automation
- Operational Automation

**3. Artificial Intelligence - 25% Weight**
- AI Strategy and Governance
- AI Model Development and Deployment
- AI Risk Management and Compliance
- AI Business Value and ROI

**4. Audit and Compliance - 25% Weight**
- Internal Audit Function
- Regulatory Compliance
- Risk Assessment and Management
- Data Governance and Controls

### Maturity Rating System

**5-Level Maturity Scale:**
- **Level 1 (1.0-1.9)**: Basic/Initial - Ad-hoc processes, limited documentation
- **Level 2 (2.0-2.9)**: Developing - Some standardization, basic controls
- **Level 3 (3.0-3.9)**: Established - Defined processes, consistent execution
- **Level 4 (4.0-4.9)**: Advanced - Optimized processes, continuous improvement
- **Level 5 (5.0)**: Leading Practice - Industry-leading, innovative approaches

## Assessment Methodology

### Comprehensive Question Framework

**560+ Detailed Questions:**
- 140 questions per domain
- 35 questions per subcategory
- Maturity-level specific criteria
- Evidence-based scoring requirements

**Question Categories:**
- Basic level assessments (1.0-1.9)
- Developing level evaluations (2.0-2.9)

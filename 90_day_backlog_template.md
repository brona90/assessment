# Truist MAPS Assessment - 90-Day Prioritized Backlog

## Backlog Creation Methodology

### Prioritization Framework
**Impact vs Effort Matrix:**
- **Quick Wins**: High Impact, Low Effort (Weeks 1-4)
- **Foundation Building**: High Impact, High Effort (Weeks 5-12)
- **Nice to Have**: Low Impact, Low Effort (Future sprints)
- **Avoid**: Low Impact, High Effort (Deprioritize)

**MAPS Alignment Scoring:**
- **Modernization**: Alignment with modernization goals (1-5)
- **Agility**: Improvement in operational agility (1-5)
- **Platforms**: Platform capability enhancement (1-5)
- **Security**: Security posture improvement (1-5)

## 90-Day Sprint Structure

### Sprint 1: Days 1-30 (Quick Wins)
**Theme**: Immediate Improvements & Foundation Setting
**Goal**: Achieve visible improvements and establish baseline

### Sprint 2: Days 31-60 (Foundation Building)
**Theme**: Core Capability Development
**Goal**: Build sustainable capabilities and processes

### Sprint 3: Days 61-90 (Optimization & Scale)
**Theme**: Process Optimization & Initial Scale
**Goal**: Optimize new capabilities and prepare for broader rollout

## Backlog Management Process

### Weekly Backlog Refinement
- **When**: Every Tuesday, 2:00 PM EST
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Participants**: Assessment team, domain owners
- **Agenda**: Review progress, adjust priorities, remove blockers

### Sprint Planning
- **Sprint 1 Planning**: Day 1 (Kickoff)
- **Sprint 2 Planning**: Day 31 (Mid-assessment)
- **Sprint 3 Planning**: Day 61 (Final phase)

### Daily Standups (During Implementation)
- **When**: Daily at 9:30 AM EST
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Focus**: Blockers, progress, coordination

### Success Measurement

#### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
1. **Completion Rate**: % of backlog items completed
2. **On-Time Delivery**: % of items delivered on schedule
3. **Quality Score**: Average quality rating of deliverables
4. **Team Satisfaction**: Internal team satisfaction scores
5. **Business Impact**: Measurable business improvements

#### Reporting Schedule
- **Weekly Progress Reports**: Every Friday
- **Sprint Retrospectives**: End of each sprint
- **Final Assessment Report**: Day 90
- **Executive Presentation**: Day 90+

This 90-day backlog will provide Truist with immediate, actionable improvements while building the foundation for long-term MAPS success.

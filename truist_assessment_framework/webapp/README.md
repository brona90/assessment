## ğŸ¨ Scoring Guide

### Maturity Levels
- **1 - Not Implemented**: Capability does not exist
- **2 - Initial**: Ad-hoc processes, limited automation
- **3 - Developing**: Defined processes, some automation
- **4 - Mature**: Well-established, mostly automated
- **5 - Optimized**: Fully automated, continuously improving

### Score Interpretation
- **0.0 - 2.0**: Significant gaps, immediate action required
- **2.1 - 3.0**: Below industry average, improvement needed
- **3.1 - 4.0**: Meeting industry standards, good progress
- **4.1 - 5.0**: Industry leading, best-in-class capabilities
